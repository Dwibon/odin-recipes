Description

Indian chicken curry is a rich and aromatic dish that embodies the essence of Indian cuisine. Made with tender pieces of chicken simmered in a flavorful sauce of tomatoes, onions, garlic, ginger, and a blend of spices like turmeric, cumin, coriander, and garam masala, it offers a perfect balance of heat and depth. Often enriched with coconut milk or yogurt for creaminess, this versatile dish varies by region, with each adding its unique twist. Served with rice or bread like naan, it’s a beloved comfort food enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • ½ cup cooking oil
  • 1 ½ cups chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 ½ teaspoons minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 (15 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
