Indian chicken curry is a rich and aromatic dish that embodies the essence of Indian cuisine. Made with tender pieces of chicken simmered in a flavorful sauce of tomatoes, onions, garlic, ginger, and a blend of spices like turmeric, cumin, coriander, and garam masala, it offers a perfect balance of heat and depth. Often enriched with coconut milk or yogurt for creaminess, this versatile dish varies by region, with each adding its unique twist.
